    Welcome to our Board Game Nights!

    Unlock Dutch language skills with our Board Game Nights! Join for an evening of fun, learning, and community. This is language education like never before. Ready to play and connect?

    A Fun Evening with a Purpose See for yourself.

    Discover how playing games can be the key to a fun and educational night! Our board game nights are specially designed to assist newcomers, immigrants, and refugees in learning the Dutch language and integrating into our society. At our events, enjoyment takes the forefront, creating a welcoming environment where everyone is encouraged to join.

    Why Participate?

    When learning Dutch, it can be challenging to engage in social situations. Our board game nights provide the perfect opportunity to casually practice Dutch while making new friends. Forget traditional language classes; here, we use games as a tool for interactive and effective learning.

    Organizers and Partners

    These board game nights are organized by a dedicated language school in collaboration with a local community association. In close cooperation with Avans University students, we've implemented an automated system to seamlessly organize these evenings. Join us and discover how enjoyable and rewarding it can be to learn the Dutch language through play!
